Organ Detoxification

Organ Detoxification

We are surrounded by toxins. They're in the air we breathe, products we put on our skin, food we eat and beverages we drink.

While your body has some of the tools it needs to flush out toxins and waste products, it can't keep up with the barrage of unavoidable toxins in today's modern American lifestyle.

Over time those toxins can build up and impair your health. Medical Director Eugene C. Rajaratnam, MD, has helped hundreds of patients detoxify their organs and improve their wellbeing.

What is organ detoxification?

Organ detoxification is the process of using evidence-based techniques to assist your body's natural toxin removal process. There are several ways to accomplish this depending on your specific needs.

To determine what is right for you, Dr. Rajaratnam will conduct a comprehensive exam and lab tests. He will inquire about your medical history, general health status and lifestyle.

Based on that he will determine the best approach to organ detox, which may include:

Colonic hydrotherapy

A gentle rinsing and flushing of your colon that gently removes toxic residue and stagnant feces to restore your digestive function and overall wellness.

Chelation
therapy

Initially used as a treatment for lead poisoning, chelating medications, delivered intravenously or in pill form, binds to heavy metals and toxins, which are then excreted through your urine.

Diet
modification

Dr. Rajaratnam can determine what changes you need to make to your diet to detox the organs harboring toxins.
