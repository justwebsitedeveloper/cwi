Dr. Eugene Rajaratnam

BOARD CERTIFIED UROLOGIST & BOARD CERTIFIED IN ANTI-AGING AND REGENERATIVE MEDICINE

Graduation

Graduated in 1976 from Stanley Medical College, Chennai India. Moved to New York and completed a Surgical and Urological Residency in 1982. Received my Board Certification in 1985.

Work

Worked as an attending Urologist in Brooklyn, NY at Coney Island Hospital until 1988, then moved to California. I have been in Private Practice in Lancaster since 1988. Founded The Antelope Valley Impotence and Incontinence Center. We treat all Urological conditions with special focus on Erectile Dysfunction and Urinary Incontinence.

Career

I was introduced to Age Management Medicine in 2007, initially trained by the Cenegenics Medical Institute in Las Vegas, NV. Since then I have been Board Certified in Anti-Aging and Regenerative Medicine through A4M.

Foundation

I also founded The California Wellness Institute (CWI). It initially started as an entity in Lancaster with plans to expand to other cities in California and internationally in the Asia Pacific Region. The Rajaratnam Foundation has been focusing in fundraising to benefit and assist underprivileged children in achieving their dreams in the Antelope Valley for the past 10 years.

Other Activities

Aside from being a physician I am also an avid pilot, photographer, and have a keen interest in Commercial Real Estate development. I am currently involved in a non-profit 501(c)(3) foundation. I Co-Produced the film “High Strung” which was released to theaters in April 2016 and is now available to stream on Netflix in HD.

Latest

My latest and current venture is the making of a Health and Wellness TV Program, “Life and How to Live It”. It will be available for viewing at LifeHowtoLiveIt.com
